Computational Neuroscience Student Initiative

The Computational Neuroscience Student Initiative is a student-led organization founded by Philipp Kathöfer and Sven Spehr at the University of Münster. Our mission is to create a collaborative space for students interested in the fascinating intersection of neuroscience and computational science. We meet every two weeks to explore and discuss cutting-edge topics in computational neuroscience. During each session, one member presents a topic they have researched, sparking discussions and fostering a deeper understanding of this rapidly evolving field.
